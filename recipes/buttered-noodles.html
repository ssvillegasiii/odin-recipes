Buttered Noodles

Description

A simple recipe with a self explanatory flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package fettuccine noodles
  • 6 tablespoons butter
  • 1/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil.
  2. Stir in fettuccine, bring back to a boil, and cook pasta over medium heat until tender yet firm to the bite, or for 8 to 10 minutes.
  3. Drain and return pasta to pot.
  4. Mix butter, Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper into pasta until evenly combined.
